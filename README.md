# 📖 Project Overview

The **Marketing Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze marketing performance across multiple campaigns, channels, regions, and product categories. It helps businesses monitor marketing effectiveness, measure campaign profitability, identify revenue-driving channels, and optimize overall marketing return on investment (ROI).

The dashboard converts raw marketing data into meaningful visual insights, enabling marketing teams and business stakeholders to make informed, data-driven decisions.

---

# 📌 Business Problem Statement

The business lacked clear visibility into its overall marketing performance. It was difficult to identify which marketing channels generated the highest revenue and conversions, evaluate campaign effectiveness, and distinguish successful campaigns from underperforming ones. Additionally, regional performance differences and customer demand across product categories were not clearly understood, making it challenging to optimize marketing strategies and maximize return on investment (ROI).

To address these challenges, this interactive Marketing Analytics Dashboard was developed to provide a centralized view of marketing performance, campaign profitability, customer engagement, and business growth.

---

The dashboard was developed to:

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- Email marketing generated the highest revenue and conversions.
- The South region produced the highest overall revenue.
- Brand Awareness Campaign achieved the highest ROI.
- Electronics contributed the largest share of total revenue.
- Revenue showed consistent year-over-year growth from 2023 to 2024.
- Summer Sale Campaign generated the highest campaign revenue.
